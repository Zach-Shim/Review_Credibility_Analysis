What is Fakeology?

Fake reviews are a prominent issue that can significantly sway consumer purchase decisions. Therefore, many sellers will deliberately manipulate these reviews on their products and services to deceive possible buyers.

Several factors contribute to biased, faked, or even automated reviews for products. Our system has three stages that utilize a number of machine learning detection algorithms to analyze the review data of a product to generate a cumulative ‘fake score’.

These steps are duplicate review detection, detection of anomalies in review count and rating distributions over time, and incentivized review detection.

This service is intended to be used as an asset for consumers when making purchase decisions.
